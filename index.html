Hi, I'm Marissa.

This is my "now page". As in – what I'm up to: now.

Right now I am learning how to code HTML and CSS in order to build this website. Hopefully, Javascript and Python will soon follow.

During my final semester at UBC, I am working with a team of engineering and business students to develop a startup in the waste management space. We are building a system of sensors installed in waste containers to serve two purposes: 1) to detect and monitor waste levels so companies can optimize pick-up routes for their on-call customers, and 2) to help them track their assets and manage inventory.

In addition to my entrepreneurial pursuits (I’m seeking other startup opportunities in Vancouver as well), I am testing for my GRE and applying for deferred admission MBA programs in the US. Rather than the GMAT, the GRE is meant to keep my other grad school options open.

I’m also getting back into all the activities I’ve postponed for the past five years of undergrad, namely: figure skating, dance (jazz, salsa, & pole – tried it twice so far), and piano. This month I had a jam session with a friend who plays the cello and I would definitely be interested in doing more duo musical adventures in general.

Currently, I am attempting to read for the third time "Great Expectations" by Charles Dickens.
